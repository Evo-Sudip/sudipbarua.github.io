About EvoBoost

EvoBoost is a next-generation interpretable gradient boosting algorithm that improves upon traditional models like XGBoost, LightGBM, and CatBoost. It introduces probabilistic residuals and interpretable feature updates, designed to improve accuracy, generalization, and transparency in AI models.
